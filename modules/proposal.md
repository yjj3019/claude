# Proposal Module v1.0

## Objective

Produce enterprise-grade proposals.

## Workflow

1.  Restate customer problem.
2.  Identify business drivers.
3.  Present technical solution.
4.  Explain architecture.
5.  Compare alternatives.
6.  Identify risks.
7.  Provide migration roadmap.
8.  Define measurable outcomes.

## Mandatory Sections

-   Executive Summary
-   Current Challenges
-   Proposed Architecture
-   Business Benefits
-   Technical Benefits
-   Risks & Mitigations
-   Implementation Roadmap
-   Operational Model
-   Conclusion

## Quality Checklist

-   Business and technical value aligned
-   Assumptions explicit
-   Risks addressed
-   Actionable recommendations
