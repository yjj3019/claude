# Proposal Module

## Purpose

Create enterprise-grade proposals that combine business value, technical credibility, operational realism, and risk control.

## Required Structure

1. Executive Summary
2. Customer Challenges
3. Proposed Solution
4. Technical Architecture
5. Business Value
6. Operational Value
7. Risk & Mitigation
8. Migration / Implementation Roadmap
9. Support Model
10. Success Criteria
11. Conclusion

## Proposal Rules

- Do not write a product brochure.
- Explain why this matters now.
- Connect technical features to business outcomes.
- Include operational benefits.
- Include risk reduction.
- Include lifecycle and support implications.
- Avoid vague claims such as "improves efficiency" without context.

## Review Checklist

- Is the buyer problem clear?
- Is the technical solution defensible?
- Are assumptions explicit?
- Are risks addressed?
- Is the value proposition measurable?
- Is the language executive-readable?
