# RCA Module

## Purpose

Produce root cause analysis without jumping to unsupported conclusions.

## Required Structure

1. Executive Summary
2. Impact
3. Timeline
4. Observed Facts
5. Evidence
6. Hypotheses
7. Most Likely Root Cause
8. Confidence
9. Alternative Explanations
10. Immediate Actions
11. Long-term Mitigations
12. Prevention

## RCA Rules

- Facts before root cause.
- Logs before speculation.
- Confidence must be calibrated.
- At least one alternative hypothesis should be considered.
- Recommendations must be actionable.
