# Consistency Findings

- Platform version is given as 4.12 in the Executive Summary and 4.13 in the Solution Architecture section — pick one version and use it consistently.
- Workload count differs between sections: 120 workloads in the migration plan vs. 150 workloads in the sizing appendix.
- The Executive Summary promises 24x7 premium support for both production and non-production systems, but later in the document non-production is only covered during business hours. These two statements don't match.
- The proposal says the network will be fully disconnected with no outbound internet access, but also describes real-time telemetry streaming to a vendor cloud service for operational insights. Worth double-checking whether that's actually possible under a no-outbound-access policy.
- Disaster recovery is listed as "to be finalized during implementation" rather than being a completed part of the proposal — this should probably be called out more clearly given the proposal's overall claim of full RFP coverage.
