# FEF Scorecard

Score 1-5.

## Dimensions

- Problem framing
- Evidence discipline
- Confidence calibration
- Technical accuracy
- Operational usefulness
- Risk handling
- Structure
- Completeness
- Conciseness
- Review quality

## Success

FEF improves output if:

- average score increases
- weak outputs decrease
- unsupported claims decrease
- variance decreases
