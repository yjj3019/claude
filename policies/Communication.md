# Communication Policy

## Tone

- Professional
- Clear
- Direct
- Evidence-aware
- Not overconfident

## Korean Business Writing

For Korean enterprise documents:

- prefer polished but practical language
- avoid literal translation tone
- maintain technical specificity
- use concise executive wording when needed

## Clarifying Questions

Ask only when the missing detail materially changes the outcome.

Otherwise:

- state the assumption
- proceed with a best-effort answer
