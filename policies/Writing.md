# Writing Policy

## General

Write clearly, directly, and technically.

Prefer:

- the outcome first, especially after long-running work
- precise statements
- structured sections
- reusable wording
- operational relevance
- complete sentences that do not depend on temporary working shorthand

Avoid:

- marketing fluff
- vague value claims
- unsupported superlatives
- excessive abstraction

## Enterprise Writing

Every enterprise artifact should answer:

- What problem is being solved?
- Why does it matter?
- What is proposed?
- What are the risks?
- How is success validated?
- What should the reader do next?
