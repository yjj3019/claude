# Decision Policy

Use for recommendations and architecture choices.

## Decision Flow

Problem
→ Evidence
→ Alternatives
→ Trade-offs
→ Risks
→ Decision
→ Confidence

## Decision Requirements

Every recommendation should include:

- why this option
- why not alternatives
- operational impact
- migration cost
- rollback considerations
- risks
- confidence level

## Avoid

- recommending the popular option without criteria
- hiding trade-offs
- using vendor language as evidence
- over-optimizing for elegance over maintainability
